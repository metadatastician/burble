== Maintainer responsibilities

Maintainers are expected to:

* Review and merge pull requests within 14 days of submission.
* Triage issues and label them appropriately within 7 days.
* Cut releases on the schedule described in `CHANGELOG.md` and `docs/compose-dialect.adoc`.
* Enforce the language policy (Rust + bash + YAML only — no Python, Go, or Node.js).
* Ensure CI stays green on `main` before any merge.

== How to become a maintainer

Open a PR adding yourself to this file. Existing maintainers vote; a simple
majority is required. You must have contributed at least three non-trivial
merged PRs before nomination.

== The lens: "the frustration without the frustration"

Every Burble capability has a *pain* and its *resolution* hiding inside it.
**Market the moment _after_ the feature — the relief the person is aching for
— never the feature itself.**

* Find the forty minutes; promise the two.
* Name the customer's own pain, and hand it back to them as relief.
* Don't sell the mechanism; sell the moment it creates.

This is the repeatable move. Apply it to *anything* Burble does.

== Principle lines (internal north stars that also read well)

* "Never break the seal to save the call." (E2EE)
* "It gets smarter while staying blind." (edge-first / ages-well moat)
* "Intent over settings." (configuration by conversation, on the right side)
* "The tool absorbs the load." (nobody the hero, nobody the scapegoat)
* "A feature that pretends to work is worse than no feature." (honesty / no half-measures)

> Burble gets you *talking and playing together in two minutes* — an AI on every
> side sorts the connection and the settings for you, privately, so nobody's
> excluded, nobody's the reluctant sysadmin, and the moment isn't ruined before
